Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our painting services

How long will my painting project take?

The duration of a painting project depends on several factors, including the size of the area, the amount of preparation required, and the specific services needed. A single room typically takes 1-2 days, while a full interior home painting project might take 3-7 days. Exterior projects usually take 3-5 days for an average-sized home. During your consultation, we'll provide a specific timeline for your project.

Do I need to move my furniture before you start painting?

We recommend moving small items and personal belongings before we arrive. For larger furniture pieces, our team can help move them to the center of the room and cover them with protective plastic sheeting. We'll discuss furniture arrangements during your consultation to ensure a smooth process.

What type of paint do you use?

We use premium quality paints from trusted brands like Sherwin-Williams and Benjamin Moore. These high-performance paints provide excellent coverage, durability, and a beautiful finish. We offer various sheens and formulations, including low-VOC and eco-friendly options for health-conscious clients.

Do you offer color consultation services?

Yes, we provide professional color consultation services to help you select the perfect colors for your space. Our experienced consultants can guide you through the selection process, considering your preferences, existing décor, lighting conditions, and the overall aesthetic you want to achieve.

What kind of warranty do you offer?

We stand behind our work with comprehensive warranties: 2 years for interior painting and 5 years for exterior painting. Our warranties cover issues such as peeling, blistering, and excessive fading. Cabinet refinishing comes with a 3-year warranty against defects in workmanship. Detailed warranty information will be provided in your project proposal.
