Cabinet Refinishing

Transform your kitchen or bathroom without the cost of a full renovation. Our cabinet refinishing services breathe new life into your existing cabinetry, delivering a fresh, modern look at a fraction of the cost of replacement.

  • Professional removal and labeling of doors and hardware
  • Thorough cleaning and degreasing of all surfaces
  • Sanding and preparation for optimal paint adhesion
  • Premium cabinet-grade paints and finishes
  • Multiple coats with sanding between applications
  • Optional hardware replacement and upgrades

Our cabinet refinishing process delivers factory-quality results that are both beautiful and durable, transforming your space while saving you thousands compared to new cabinets.
