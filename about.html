About Precision Finish

Learn about our story, our team, and our commitment to excellence

Our Story

Precision Finish was founded by Nic Guedenet, a professional with deep expertise in the painting and finishing industry. The name "Precision Finish" reflects our core commitment to delivering meticulous craftsmanship and attention to detail in every project we undertake.

Although Precision Finish is a newly launched brand, our founding team brings over 40 years of combined experience in the painting and construction industry. This level of experience brings unmatched reliability, industry insight, and time-tested methods that ensure exceptional outcomes on every job.

In the construction and renovation world, high-end finish work is often the most overlooked yet most impactful aspect. Precision Finish was created specifically to fill this gap. What sets us apart is our unwavering dedication to superior craftsmanship, attention to detail, and a client-first approach.

We treat every home or building like it's our own, delivering results that exceed expectations and elevate property value. Our comprehensive range of services includes interior and exterior painting, kitchen cabinet refinishing, home exterior repair, wall paneling installation, interior trim installation, wainscoting, and custom carpentry.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver elite-quality craftsmanship with integrity, precision, and professionalism—bringing beauty and longevity to every project we undertake.

We are dedicated to redefining excellence in finish work by providing superior painting, finishes, coatings, wall coverings, and comprehensive interior and exterior renovation services that transform spaces and exceed client expectations.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the premier provider of high-quality painting and finishing services in Roseville, Rocklin, Granite Bay, and Lincoln, California, known for our exceptional attention to detail and superior craftsmanship.

We aim to raise the bar and redefine quality in painting and restoration services, becoming the trusted partner for both residential homeowners seeking to beautify their living spaces and commercial property owners requiring professional, high-quality finish work.
