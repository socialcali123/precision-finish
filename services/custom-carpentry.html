Our Custom Carpentry Process

A collaborative approach that brings your vision to life

1

Initial Consultation

We begin with a thorough consultation to understand your vision, needs, and goals. We discuss your ideas, preferences, and how you plan to use the space, while also assessing the architectural context and taking preliminary measurements.

2

Design Development

Based on our consultation, we develop a custom design concept for your carpentry project. This includes detailed sketches or renderings, material recommendations, and finish options that bring your vision to life while addressing practical considerations.

3

Detailed Measurements

We take precise measurements of your space, accounting for all architectural elements, utilities, and other features that may impact the design. This ensures a perfect fit and seamless integration with your existing interior.

4

Material Selection

We help you select the perfect materials for your project, considering factors such as appearance, durability, maintenance requirements, and budget. From hardwoods and veneers to hardware and finishes, every component is carefully chosen.

5

Detailed Proposal

We provide a comprehensive proposal that includes final designs, material specifications, timeline, and transparent pricing. We review this with you in detail, making any necessary adjustments to ensure the project perfectly meets your expectations.

6

Fabrication

Our skilled craftsmen begin building your custom carpentry in our workshop. We use premium materials and traditional woodworking techniques combined with modern tools to ensure precision and quality. Complex pieces are often pre-assembled to verify fit and finish.

7

Professional Installation

Our experienced installation team carefully transports and installs your custom carpentry with meticulous attention to detail. We ensure everything is perfectly level, securely anchored, and properly finished, with minimal disruption to your home.

8

Finishing Touches

Once installation is complete, we apply any on-site finishes, adjust hardware, and ensure all components function perfectly. We pay special attention to details like trim work, edge treatments, and seamless transitions that elevate the final result.

9

Final Inspection & Cleanup

We conduct a thorough inspection to ensure every detail meets our high standards of quality and craftsmanship. The work area is completely cleaned, debris is removed, and we walk through the project with you to ensure your complete satisfaction.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our custom carpentry services

How long does a custom carpentry project take?

The timeline varies depending on the scope and complexity of the project. Simple built-ins might take 2-3 weeks from design to installation, while more extensive or intricate projects can take 4-8 weeks or longer. During our consultation, we'll provide a specific timeline based on your project's requirements and our current schedule.

What types of wood do you work with?

We work with a wide variety of hardwoods and softwoods including oak, maple, cherry, walnut, mahogany, pine, and poplar. We also work with engineered wood products like medium-density fiberboard (MDF) and plywood when appropriate. The best choice depends on your design preferences, budget, and the specific requirements of your project.

Can you match existing woodwork in my home?

Yes, we specialize in matching existing woodwork to maintain consistency throughout your home. Our craftsmen can replicate most styles, profiles, and finishes, even if they're no longer commercially available. For historic homes, we take special care to ensure period-appropriate details that preserve your home's architectural integrity.

Do you provide design services or should I have my own design?

We offer comprehensive design services as part of our custom carpentry process. While you're welcome to bring your own designs or inspiration, our team can develop custom designs based on your ideas and needs. We'll work collaboratively with you to create a solution that perfectly fits your space and style preferences.

How do I maintain my custom woodwork?

Maintenance requirements depend on the type of wood and finish used. Generally, regular dusting with a soft cloth and occasional cleaning with appropriate wood cleaners is sufficient. For stained or natural wood, periodic application of furniture polish or wax may be recommended. We'll provide specific care instructions based on your project's materials and finishes to ensure long-lasting beauty.
