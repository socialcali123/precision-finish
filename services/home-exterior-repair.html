Why timely exterior repairs are essential for your home

Prevent Costly Damage

Addressing exterior issues early prevents them from developing into major structural problems that require expensive repairs.

Our Exterior Repair Process

A systematic approach to restoring your home's exterior

1

Comprehensive Inspection

We begin with a thorough inspection of your home's exterior, identifying all damage, deterioration, and potential problem areas. Our experts examine siding, trim, fascia, soffits, windows, doors, and structural elements to create a complete assessment.

2

Detailed Proposal

Based on our inspection, we provide a detailed proposal outlining all necessary repairs, recommended solutions, material options, timeline, and transparent pricing. We take time to explain our findings and answer any questions you may have.

3

Preparation

Before beginning repairs, we properly prepare the work area by protecting landscaping, removing obstacles, and setting up necessary equipment. For extensive repairs, we may remove damaged materials to fully assess the extent of underlying issues.

4

Structural Repairs

We address any structural issues first, replacing damaged framing, reinforcing weakened areas, and ensuring a solid foundation for exterior finishes. Our carpenters use premium pressure-treated lumber and proper techniques to ensure lasting structural integrity.

5

Siding and Trim Repair

We repair or replace damaged siding, trim, fascia, and soffits using materials that match your existing exterior. Whether it's wood, fiber cement, vinyl, or composite materials, we ensure seamless integration with your home's current appearance.

6

Weather Protection

We install proper flashing, caulking, and weather-resistant barriers to protect against water infiltration and weather damage. These critical details prevent future problems and extend the life of your exterior finishes.

7

Surface Preparation & Finishing

All repaired surfaces are properly prepared for finishing through cleaning, sanding, and priming. We then apply premium paints or stains that provide both protection and beautiful aesthetics, ensuring a cohesive look across your entire exterior.

8

Final Inspection & Cleanup

We conduct a thorough inspection of all completed repairs to ensure everything meets our high standards. The work area is completely cleaned, debris is removed, and we walk through the project with you to ensure your complete satisfaction.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our exterior repair services

How do I know if my home needs exterior repairs?

Signs that your home may need exterior repairs include visible cracks or gaps in siding, peeling or bubbling paint, rotting or soft wood, water stains on interior walls, increased energy bills, visible mold or mildew, and damaged or missing trim. If you notice any of these issues, it's best to have a professional assessment to prevent further damage.

Can you match my existing siding and trim when making repairs?

Yes, we specialize in matching existing materials and finishes to ensure repairs blend seamlessly with your home's current appearance. Our team has extensive experience working with all types of siding materials including wood, vinyl, fiber cement, and composite products.

How long do exterior repairs typically take to complete?

The timeline varies depending on the scope and complexity of the repairs needed. Minor repairs may be completed in 1-2 days, while more extensive restoration projects can take 1-2 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during the proposal stage and keep you informed of our progress throughout the project.

Do you provide warranties on exterior repairs?

Yes, we stand behind our work with a comprehensive 5-year warranty on all exterior repairs. This covers any defects in workmanship or material failure under normal conditions. Additionally, many of the materials we use come with manufacturer warranties that we pass on to you.

Can exterior repairs be done in winter or rainy weather?

While we can perform some repairs year-round, certain aspects of exterior work require specific weather conditions. Temperature and moisture levels can affect material installation and finishing. We schedule work during appropriate weather windows to ensure optimal results and will communicate any weather-related delays.
