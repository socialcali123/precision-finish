Our Cabinet Refinishing Process

A meticulous approach that ensures exceptional results

1

Consultation & Assessment

We begin with a thorough consultation to understand your vision and goals. Our experts will assess your existing cabinets, discuss color and finish options, and provide professional recommendations tailored to your specific needs and preferences.

2

Preparation & Removal

We carefully remove all cabinet doors, drawers, and hardware, labeling each piece to ensure proper reinstallation. We protect your countertops, floors, and surrounding areas to prevent any damage during the refinishing process.

3

Cleaning & Degreasing

Kitchen cabinets accumulate grease, dirt, and residue over time. We thoroughly clean and degrease all surfaces to ensure proper adhesion of the new finish, paying special attention to areas near the stove and high-touch points.

4

Sanding & Surface Preparation

We meticulously sand all surfaces to create the ideal foundation for the new finish. This step removes the existing finish, smooths imperfections, and creates a surface that will ensure optimal adhesion and durability of the new finish.

5

Priming

We apply a high-quality, adhesion-promoting primer specifically designed for cabinet surfaces. This crucial step ensures excellent coverage, prevents bleed-through, and creates the perfect base for the topcoat.

6

Painting & Finishing

Using premium cabinet-grade paints or finishes, we apply multiple coats with careful sanding between applications. Our professional spray application ensures a smooth, factory-like finish free of brush marks or roller texture.

7

Reassembly & Hardware Installation

Once the finish has properly cured, we carefully reinstall all cabinet doors, drawers, and hardware. We can also install new hardware if you've chosen to update these elements as part of your kitchen transformation.

8

Final Inspection & Cleanup

We conduct a thorough inspection to ensure every detail meets our high standards of quality. We make any necessary adjustments to ensure proper alignment and function, then complete a comprehensive cleanup, leaving your kitchen ready to enjoy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our cabinet refinishing services

How long does cabinet refinishing take?

The typical timeline for a standard kitchen cabinet refinishing project is 5-7 days from start to finish. This can vary based on the size of your kitchen, the number of cabinets, and the complexity of the project.

How durable is the new cabinet finish?

Our professional-grade finishes are extremely durable and designed specifically for high-use cabinet surfaces. With proper care, your refinished cabinets will maintain their beauty and integrity for many years, standing up to daily use in a busy kitchen environment.

Can you change the cabinet style or just the color?

While refinishing primarily changes the color and finish of your cabinets, we can also make some style modifications such as adding decorative molding, changing door panels, or updating hardware to give your cabinets a completely new look.

Do you offer a warranty on cabinet refinishing?

Yes, we stand behind our work with a comprehensive 2-year warranty on all cabinet refinishing projects. This covers any issues with the finish such as peeling, chipping, or cracking under normal use conditions.

Can I use my kitchen during the refinishing process?

You will have limited access to your kitchen during the refinishing process. While you won't be able to use the cabinets, you will typically have access to your sink, refrigerator, and other appliances. We work efficiently to minimize disruption to your daily routine.
