Wall Paneling Styles

Explore our range of custom paneling options for your home

Board and Batten

A classic style featuring vertical boards with thin strips (battens) covering the seams. This versatile design adds texture and dimension, working beautifully in entryways, dining rooms, and bedrooms.

Shiplap

Horizontal wooden boards installed with a slight overlap, creating distinctive shadow lines. This popular style offers a clean, contemporary look with subtle texture, perfect for modern farmhouse and coastal interiors.

Beadboard

Featuring narrow vertical planks with rounded grooves (beads) between each board. This charming, traditional style adds cottage-inspired character to kitchens, bathrooms, and mudrooms.

Raised Panel

Elegant and formal, raised panel designs feature beveled edges that create a sophisticated, three-dimensional effect. Ideal for formal dining rooms, libraries, and traditional living spaces.

Geometric Patterns

Modern and eye-catching, geometric paneling creates bold visual interest with diamond, herringbone, or custom patterns. Perfect for accent walls and contemporary spaces seeking dramatic impact.

Reclaimed Wood

Sustainable and full of character, reclaimed wood paneling brings warmth, history, and unique texture to any space. Each installation tells a story while adding rustic charm to modern interiors.

The Benefits of Wall Paneling

Why custom wall paneling is a valuable addition to your home

Enhanced Aesthetics

Wall paneling adds architectural interest, texture, and dimension that transforms plain walls into stunning design features.

Increased Home Value

Quality architectural details like custom wall paneling are desirable features that can significantly increase your property's market value.

Wall Protection

Paneling provides an extra layer of protection against scuffs, dents, and everyday wear, particularly in high-traffic areas.

Improved Acoustics

Wood paneling can help absorb sound and reduce echo, creating a more comfortable acoustic environment in your home.

Added Insulation

Wall paneling provides an additional layer of insulation that can help improve energy efficiency and comfort.

Conceals Imperfections

Paneling is an excellent solution for covering wall imperfections or uneven surfaces without extensive repairs.

Our Wall Paneling Installation Process

A meticulous approach that ensures exceptional results

1

Consultation & Design

We begin with a thorough consultation to understand your vision, preferences, and goals. Our design team will help you select the perfect paneling style, material, and finish to complement your space and achieve your desired aesthetic.

2

Detailed Measurements

We take precise measurements of your space, accounting for all architectural elements, electrical outlets, switches, and other features. This ensures a perfect fit and seamless integration with your existing interior.

3

Material Selection & Preparation

Based on your design choices, we select premium materials that will deliver the desired look and durability. All materials are carefully prepared, cut to precise specifications, and, if necessary, pre-finished before installation begins.

4

Wall Preparation

We thoroughly prepare your walls to ensure a solid foundation for the paneling. This includes cleaning, repairing any damage, and ensuring the surface is level. Proper preparation is crucial for a flawless, long-lasting installation.

5

Professional Installation

Our skilled craftsmen install your wall paneling with meticulous attention to detail. We use proper techniques and tools to ensure precise alignment, secure attachment, and perfect finishing at edges, corners, and around obstacles.

6

Finishing Touches

Once the paneling is installed, we apply any necessary finishing touches such as caulking seams, filling nail holes, and sanding edges for a seamless appearance. This attention to detail ensures a professional, polished result.

7

Painting or Staining

If your paneling requires on-site finishing, our expert painters apply premium paints or stains to achieve your desired color and finish. We use proper techniques to ensure even coverage and a beautiful, durable result.

8

Final Inspection & Cleanup

We conduct a thorough inspection to ensure every detail meets our high standards of quality. The work area is completely cleaned, debris is removed, and we walk through the project with you to ensure your complete satisfaction.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our wall paneling installation services

How long does wall paneling installation take?

The timeline varies depending on the size of the area and complexity of the design. A single accent wall might be completed in 1-2 days, while more extensive installations covering multiple rooms can take 3-5 days. We'll provide a specific timeline during your consultation based on your project's scope.

What materials do you use for wall paneling?

We work with a variety of high-quality materials including solid wood, medium-density fiberboard (MDF), PVC, and composite materials. The best choice depends on your design preferences, budget, and the specific requirements of your space. Our team will help you select the ideal material for your project.

Can wall paneling be installed in bathrooms or other moisture-prone areas?

Yes, wall paneling can be installed in bathrooms and other moisture-prone areas when the appropriate materials and finishes are used. For these spaces, we recommend moisture-resistant options like PVC panels, properly sealed wood, or specially treated MDF, along with moisture-resistant paints and sealants.

Do you offer custom designs or only standard patterns?

We specialize in both standard and custom wall paneling designs. While we offer popular styles like board and batten, shiplap, and traditional wainscoting, we also create completely custom designs tailored to your specific vision and space. Our craftsmen can execute virtually any paneling concept you have in mind.

How do I maintain and clean my wall paneling?

Maintenance is generally simple and depends on the material and finish. Most painted paneling can be cleaned with a damp cloth and mild soap. Wood paneling may require occasional dusting and periodic treatment with appropriate wood cleaners or polishes. We'll provide specific care instructions for your particular installation.
